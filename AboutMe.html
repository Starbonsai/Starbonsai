I'm Kristen, an American-Philipino artist, but most call me 'Star' or 'Bonsai'. I love to draw, play games, and study animal biology in my free time.

As an artist, I think my strengths are color and character design- it's also something I try to improve and learn more about every day. My main inspirations are from real life animal biology, alien-monster characters, and cartoons. I grew up as a big fan of shows from Cartoon Network, Disney, and Nickolodeon, and you can see some of my design inspiration come from their cartoons.
My best pieces of art are done when I am listening to my favorite music genres- future funk, Italo-Disco, House, EDM, citypop, alternate rock, video game music, and anything in-between.
I'm always trying to use nostalgic and fun color palettes in my pieces, and I will strive to come up with more color palettes for the future.

Another way to get to know me is by understanding what makes me laugh. I find my sense of humor comes from places of familiarity- Oneyplays, SrPelo, Smiling Friends, and Futurama are all inspirations for me and my growing Youtube Channel. I'm trying to get better at making youtube videos, and I hope eventually I can find an audience that enjoys my kind of content. As someone who plays games in her free time, I find that I mostly enjoy those on Switch and PC. Games such as Splatoon, Bayonetta, Terraria, Pokemon, and The Super Mario franchise are also inspirations you can see in my artwork. After a long day of work, I like playing games like these to unwind (I'm also quite competitve!).
